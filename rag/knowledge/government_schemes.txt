GOVERNMENT SCHEMES FOR NATURAL AND ORGANIC FARMING IN INDIA — 2024-25

PM-KISAN SAMMAN NIDHI YOJANA
Full Name: Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi
Annual Benefit: ₹6,000 per year (₹2,000 in 3 equal installments)
Eligibility: All small and marginal farmers owning cultivable land
How to Apply: 
  1. Visit nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or agricultural office
  2. Register on pmkisan.gov.in
  3. Documents: Aadhaar card, land records (Khasra/Khatauni), bank account
Status Check: pmkisan.gov.in/BeneficiaryStatus.aspx
Helpline: 155261 / 1800-115-526 (Toll Free)
Source: Department of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare

PARAMPARAGAT KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (PKVY)
Full Name: Traditional Agriculture Development Scheme
Focus: Organic and Natural Farming
Benefit: ₹50,000 per hectare over 3 years for cluster-based organic certification
- Year 1: ₹20,000/ha (soil testing, input purchase, training)
- Year 2: ₹15,000/ha (continuity support)
- Year 3: ₹15,000/ha (marketing support, certification assistance)
Eligibility: Farmer clusters of minimum 50 farmers, 50+ acres together
Application: Through District Agricultural Officer → State Agriculture Department
Training: Mandatory PKVY training provided before grant release
Website: pgsindia-ncof.gov.in
Helpline: 011-23382557

PM FASAL BIMA YOJANA (PMFBY)
Full Name: Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (Crop Insurance)
Premium Rates:
  - Kharif crops: 2% of Sum Insured
  - Rabi crops: 1.5% of Sum Insured
  - Annual commercial/horticulture crops: 5% of Sum Insured
  Remaining premium paid by Central + State government
Coverage: Sowing failure, standing crop losses, post-harvest losses
How to Apply: Through bank (where KCC loan was taken) or insurance companies
Website: pmfby.gov.in
Helpline: 1800-200-7710

NATIONAL MISSION FOR SUSTAINABLE AGRICULTURE (NMSA)
Sub-mission: Soil Health Management
Benefits:
  - Soil Health Cards distributed free
  - Micro-irrigation subsidy: 55% for small/marginal farmers
  - Organic farming cluster development support
  - On-farm water management infrastructure support
Application: Through State Agriculture Department
Website: agricoop.nic.in

PM KISAN MANDHAN YOJANA (PMKMY)
Benefit: Pension of ₹3,000/month after age 60
Eligibility: Small and marginal farmers aged 18–40 years
Monthly Contribution (matched by government):
  - 18 years: ₹55/month
  - 30 years: ₹110/month  
  - 40 years: ₹200/month
Application: CSC centres or online at maandhan.in
Helpline: 1800-267-6888

KISAN CREDIT CARD (KCC)
Benefit: Short-term credit at 4% interest per year (normally 7% — 3% subvention by govt)
Credit Limit: Based on land holding and crop requirement — typically ₹50,000 to ₹3 lakh
Eligibility: All farmers, tenant farmers, sharecroppers
How to Apply: Visit bank branch with: Aadhaar, land documents, two passport photos
Special: KCC holders get priority for PMFBY crop insurance enrollment

RASHTRIYA KRISHI VIKAS YOJANA (RKVY)
Focus: Agricultural infrastructure and innovation
Natural Farming Support: State-specific grants for ZBNF clusters
Eligibility: Applied through state governments — farmer groups preferred
Benefit: Variable — typically ₹2,000–5,000/acre for first 2 years

ANDHRA PRADESH — COMMUNITY MANAGED NATURAL FARMING (APCNF)
State Specific (Andhra Pradesh only)
Benefit: Free training, inputs support, and market linkage for NF farmers
Contact: Dr.Y.S.R. State Institute of Agro Industries, Amaravati
Website: apagros.ap.gov.in

SOIL HEALTH CARD SCHEME
Free soil testing every 2 years
Shows: N, P, K, pH, micronutrient deficiencies
How to Get: Visit nearest Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK) or Agricultural office
Online Soil Health Card: soilhealth.dac.gov.in

NATIONAL BEE BOARD SCHEME (Natural Farming Synergy)
Benefit: ₹40 per bee colony installed in farm, training on integrated bee-keeping
Available: For farmers who adopt natural farming and have orchards/vegetables

PM KRISHI SINCHAI YOJANA — DRIP IRRIGATION SUBSIDY
Subsidy: 55% for small/marginal, 45% for large farmers
Coverage: Drip systems, sprinkler systems, micro-irrigation
Application: State Agriculture Engineering Department
Website: pmksy.gov.in

eNAM — NATIONAL AGRICULTURE MARKET
Platform: Electronic National Agriculture Market — sells produce online to buyers across India
Fee: Zero registration, 1% transaction fee on sale
How to Register: Through local APMC mandi
Website: enam.gov.in
Benefits for NF Farmers: Premium pricing for certified organic produce possible

IMPORTANT NOTES:
1. Scheme amounts and eligibility criteria may change annually — always verify from official websites
2. Most schemes require Aadhaar-linked bank account for direct benefit transfer
3. For any scheme disputes: PM-KISAN helpline 155261, Kisan Call Centre 1800-180-1551
4. Farmer Producer Organisations (FPOs) get additional benefits under most schemes

Source: Ministry of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare, GOI 2024; State Agriculture Department Circulars
