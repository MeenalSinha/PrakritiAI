BEEJAMRUT — NATURAL SEED TREATMENT FOR HEALTHY GERMINATION

What is Beejamrut?
Beejamrut (Seed Nectar) is a natural seed treatment solution used in Zero Budget Natural Farming to protect seeds from soil-borne diseases and improve germination vigor. It replaces chemical fungicides and seed treatments completely.

Standard Beejamrut Recipe (For 1 kg seeds):
- 50 grams fresh desi cow dung
- 50 ml fresh desi cow urine  
- 1 small limestone (chuna) piece — dissolved in water overnight to make lime water
- 1 liter clean water
- A small pinch of soil from a healthy field bund

Simple Recipe for Farmers (Per 10 kg seeds):
1. Take 5 liters water in a bucket
2. Add 500g cow dung (dissolved well)
3. Add 500ml cow urine
4. Add 50g lime water (chuna pani)
5. Stir well and leave for 12 hours
6. Strain through cloth and the liquid is your Beejamrut

How to Use:
METHOD 1 — Seed Soaking:
- Place seeds in jute sack or flat surface
- Pour Beejamrut solution over seeds
- Mix gently to coat all seeds
- Let seeds absorb for 20–30 minutes
- Spread in shade for drying (1–2 hours)
- Never dry in direct sunlight after treatment
- Sow within 6–8 hours of treatment

METHOD 2 — Quick Coating:
- Spread seeds thin on plastic sheet
- Spray Beejamrut uniformly
- Turn seeds and spray other side
- Allow to air dry in shade for 2 hours
- Sow immediately

Crops and Benefits:
Paddy/Rice: Protects against blast, sheath blight, and BPH damage
Wheat: Protects against loose smut, Karnal bunt
Pulses (Moong, Urad, Chana): Prevents seed-borne collar rot
Cotton: Protects against damping off, seedling blight
Soybean: Prevents Phytophthora root rot
Vegetables: Universal protection against soil-borne pathogens

Why Beejamrut Works:
1. Lime (calcium hydroxide) is alkaline — neutralizes fungal spores on seed surface
2. Cow dung microbes colonize seed coat — competitive exclusion of pathogens
3. Cow urine contains uric acid — natural anti-fungal, anti-bacterial
4. The combination creates a microbial shield that travels with the seed into soil

Expected Results:
- Germination rate increases 10–20% compared to untreated seeds
- Seedling mortality from damping-off nearly eliminated
- Stronger root development in first 15 days
- 7–10 day reduction in time to first true leaves

After Beejamrut, the Next Step is Jeevamrut:
Apply Jeevamrut as soil drench on Day 15 after sowing for best results.

Storage:
- Beejamrut should be used within 24 hours of preparation
- Do not store more than 1 day — microbes die
- Prepare fresh for each sowing batch

Important Notes:
- Always use desi cow products — not buffalo or Jersey cow
- Pregnant buffaloes' urine should not be used
- Lime must be properly dissolved — direct application burns seeds
- Do not mix with chemical pesticides before or after treatment
